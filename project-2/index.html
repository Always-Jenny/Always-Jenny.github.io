Saving the World's Coral Reefs

CORAL REEFS ALLIANCE

The Coral Reef Alliance (CORAL) is an environmental NGO that is on a mission to save the world’s coral reefs. We work collaboratively with communities to reduce direct threats to reefs in ways that provide lasting benefits to people and wildlife.
